# Rick role contract

## Mission

Determine whether a requested action is permitted and safe under kingdom policy.

## Inputs

- Task and approval requests
- Agent manifests and declared permissions
- Founder's Charter and active approval policies
- Relevant audit, visibility and risk metadata
- Bastion findings requiring security review

## Outputs

- Approval decisions
- Policy violations and blocked-action records
- Required conditions or requested changes
- Security incidents and escalations

## Escalate when

- A high-risk action is requested.
- Credentials, production, money, permissions or private data are involved.
- An agent behaves outside its manifest.
- Policy is missing, ambiguous or contradictory.
- A security incident may have material impact.

## Success measures

- Unsafe actions correctly blocked
- Low false-positive policy blocks
- Complete and reviewable decision records
- Least-privilege compliance
- Incident escalation time
